About Us

The big idea behind Orbitz is very simple. We love asking questions and figuring out how things work. That curiosity helped us build a drink that looks super clean and tastes incredibly delicious. We want you to feel that same excitement when you learn about science. To help make that happen, we team up with SpaceKids Global. They run amazing programs that teach kids how to explore the galaxy and understand space travel. We mix the drinks, and they help you reach the stars. Together, we hope to inspire the next generation of astronauts and scientists.
