FAQ

Can We Customize Our Stay (Add Wine, Flowers, or Extra Nights.)?

Absolutely! You can add romantic extras, such as wine service or floral arrangements, and extend your stay through our booking area, after selecting the package you would like to add.

Do The Couples Massages Need to Be Booked In Advance?

Yes! Spa appointments fill quickly during Valentine's week, so we recommend booking your preferred time when reserving your stay.

What Is The Cancellation Policy?

Free cancellations up to 72 hours before check-in. Cancellations within 72 hours will incur a $33 CAD fee (equivalent to one night of the package).
