FAQ

Do Packages Cover All-Inclusive Perks?

Yes, all packages include full access to the All-Inclusive perks.

Do You Accommodate Airplanes with Floats?

Yes! To arrange a float plane accommodation:

  1. Reserve your spot the day before or day of arrival.
  2. We'll assist with docking.
  3. Please note: space is limited, as we are a small resort.
Are There Any Extra Fees?

13% HST TAX, Lunches, Extra food, Spa Treatments (however, repeat guests receive 2 free 1/2 treatments if here in the last 2 years), 20/30/40 hpr Italian Deck Boat(s), Drinks from the Bar, Store, Parking $20/Night. Spa Products.
